The Department of Computer Science and Engineering focuses on establishing a strong foundation in fundamental concepts like logical thinking , instilling programming skills in the minds of the students, and exposing day-to-day developing trends in computer science and engineering like SMAC technology (Social network analysis, Mobile computing, Analytics and Cloud computing) and Quantum computing. Also, through the students' association, workshop and leadership programmes, the students are motivated to develop organizational and leadership skills apart from mastering technical skills. Our experienced and dedicated faculty encourage the learning habits of the students inside and outside the class room as well as encourage peer-learning. Our aim is to provide technical and soft skills to our students with a view towards placements in reputed national and international organizations; and also, we encourage students to involve in all domains of co-curricular activities so that our students can blend in and tackle any challenge in their chosen field.


Projects are how ideas become reality. As much as academics is
important , projects can be one of the most efficient way to learn as they encourage the students to apply the skills while learning them. This aids in retention and increases the usefulness of the skills learnt. They allow the students to gain recognition, being one of the most powerful tools in the arsenal of things to do with their time. There is a certain logic behind every project and our students have their own.
